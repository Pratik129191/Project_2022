Super Specialized Services

Blood Cancers

LifeCell has complete range of modalities for diagnosis of blood cancer using blood, bone marrow and lymph node samples. Our Haemato-oncology department offers state of art facilities for diagnosing all kinds of Haemato-Lymphoid and Lymphatic disorders . Our reference lab has IVD approved Flow cytometer ( 10 colors and three laser flow cytometer ) for characterization and detection of Minimal Residual Disease (MRD) in Leukemias/Lymphomas.

Newborn Screening

Newborn Screening program helps in detecting more than 55 congenital disorders in newborns before they become symptomatic. It helps in early diagnosis and treatment of the disorder. If left untreated and undiagnosed, many of the disorders can lead to severe morbidity and mortality.

Immuno Assays

We at LifeCell Diagnostic & Research Centre know how important it is for patients to receive a fast and accurate diagnosis. Our services reduce waiting times while increasing choice and convenience. Our highly trained clinical teams are professional and compassionate, dedicated to providing the highest standard of service.

Genetic Disorders

Genetic Disorders are diseases caused by changes in the genetic makeup of the individual. Though, these disorders are rare, but these disorders need to be diagnosed for knowing the disease, its treatment, prognosis, as well as its prevention in future pregnancies.

Histopathology / Biopsy

All kinds of biopsies and surgical specimens are tested at our laboratory. Along with biopsy testing, we also offer ancillary testing services like Immunohistochemistry (IHC), Fluoroscent In-situ Hybridization (FISH) and Molecular testing services. All cancer reports are generated on International guidelines. Critical reports are informed to the client. All the reports are accompanied with high definition microphotographs .

Reproductive Health Test

At Modern, we offer all kind of test services in Reproductive Health segment. Besides the specialized test services and panels, we also have a specialized Reprogenetics lab for genetic testing. We have a highly qualified team of experts who handle the specimens with great care and skills. Our aim is to help deliver healthy babies with the help of high quality diagnostic services and testing.

Heart or Cardiovascular diseases

The cardiac pathology team is highly experienced in the interpretation of endomyocardial biopsies from both cardiac allografts and native hearts. This team is also responsible for diagnostic interpretation on other surgical pathology specimens obtained from the heart and blood vessels.

Neurological Diagnostic Tests

All aspects of diagnostic neuropathology service are provided. Active Neurosurgery and Neurology Departments, as well as an active outside diagnostic consultation service, provide over 500 neurosurgical specimens and over 150 muscle and nerve biopsies each year. About 150 autopsies are performed yearly at our three affiliated hospitals and all brains are reviewed by neuropsychologist.

Gastrointestinal & Liver Pathology

Our goals in the Gastrointestinal, Liver, and Pancreas Pathology Laboratory are to provide excellent diagnostic service, to spearhead and contribute to innovative research, and to educate students, trainees, and colleagues. The scope of expertise and research covered by our faculty members encompasses inflammatory nonneoplastic conditions and tumors of the digestive system: gastrointestinal tract, pancreas, biliary tract, and liver.

Pediatric solid organ neoplasia, with active participation in the Children's Oncology Group (COG)

The Division of Pediatric Pathology offers a full array of state of the art diagnostic services in the areas of Anatomic and Clinical Pathology for patients. Consultative services at the national and international levels are also provided. Classic and modern techniques are available, including an extensive immunohistochemistry menu, in situ hybridization and ultrastructural pathology.

Opthalmic Pathology that focuses on diseases of the eye and its neighboring tissues.

The Department of Eye Pathology (DEP) is a National Specialist Ophthalmic Pathology Service (NSOPS) laboratory and offers a highly specialised clinical laboratory service. We are a patient-centric department and we regularly exceed our stated target turnaround time of 90% of samples received are reported in 7 days or less.
